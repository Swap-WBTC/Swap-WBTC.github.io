What is Swap WBTC?

Uniswap is a self-custody app destination for trading ERC-20 assets directly from a connected wallet. WBTC is Wrapped Bitcoin on Ethereum, and its token contract can be checked on Etherscan.

Swap WBTC on Uniswap

To swap WBTC on Uniswap, use the official Uniswap web app, select Ethereum mainnet, and verify that Wrapped Bitcoin is the token at 0x2260FAC5E5542a773Aa44fBCfeDf7C193bc2C599. WBTC is an ERC-20 representation of Bitcoin on Ethereum, so a Uniswap trade exchanges an Ethereum asset such as ETH, USDC, or WETH for WBTC; it does not mint native BTC or redeem BTC from custody. The quote should show route, estimated output, price impact, slippage settings, app fees if any, and Ethereum gas. Because WBTC has 8 decimals and a high unit price, always verify the amount and avoid relying on token symbols alone.

Swap WBTC fees price impact and slippage

Swap WBTC fees on Uniswap are not a single fixed number. The final execution can include liquidity-pool fees, Uniswap interface or routing behavior where applicable, Ethereum gas, and price impact from trade size relative to pool liquidity. Price impact is especially important for large WBTC trades because even a small percentage difference can be meaningful in dollar terms. Slippage tolerance controls how much worse the final output can be before the transaction reverts, but raising it too far can expose the trade to poor execution. A user should compare the quoted WBTC amount, minimum received, route, gas estimate, and price impact warning before signing.

Swap WBTC token contract and network

The canonical WBTC route for this page is WBTC on Ethereum mainnet, contract 0x2260FAC5E5542a773Aa44fBCfeDf7C193bc2C599. Token symbols can be copied by unrelated contracts, and WBTC-like assets also exist on other networks or bridges, so contract and network checks are part of the swap intent. On Ethereum, gas is paid in native ETH, not WBTC. If the wallet holds WBTC on another chain, the user needs a bridge or that chain's supported swap route, not an Ethereum mainnet Uniswap transaction. Etherscan, CoinGecko, and the official WBTC site are useful references before approving a high-value token.

Swap WBTC to ETH or stablecoins

Swapping WBTC to ETH, WETH, USDC, or another stablecoin on Uniswap follows the same wallet-signed flow: choose WBTC as input, select the output asset, review the quote, approve WBTC if needed, and confirm the swap. The key search questions are approval, gas, and minimum received. An approval transaction only grants spending permission; the swap itself is a separate transaction unless the app can use an existing approval path. WBTC's market price tracks Bitcoin exposure, while stablecoin output depends on pool liquidity and route quality. For larger trades, splitting orders or comparing venues may reduce price impact, but every added route creates its own execution risk.

Swap WBTC FAQ

What is the WBTC contract for a Uniswap swap?

On Ethereum mainnet, the WBTC token contract is 0x2260FAC5E5542a773Aa44fBCfeDf7C193bc2C599. Check it on Etherscan or a trusted token index before approving a swap, because unrelated tokens can copy the WBTC symbol.

Do I need BTC to swap WBTC?

No. A Uniswap WBTC swap can use an Ethereum asset such as ETH, WETH, USDC, or another supported ERC-20 token as input. Minting or redeeming WBTC with native BTC is a separate process from a DEX swap.

Why is WBTC not exactly the same price as BTC in my quote?

A swap quote reflects available liquidity, route, fees, price impact, and current market conditions. WBTC is designed to represent Bitcoin on Ethereum, but DEX execution can differ from a spot BTC price shown on a centralized exchange or data site.

Can I swap WBTC without ETH for gas?

On Ethereum mainnet, you need native ETH to pay gas even if the trade uses WBTC and another ERC-20 token. If your wallet has WBTC but no ETH, the swap transaction cannot execute until gas is funded.

Is swapping WBTC taxable?

Tax treatment depends on jurisdiction, but token-to-token swaps are often reportable events. The landing page should not give tax advice. Users should preserve transaction hashes, token amounts, timestamps, and cost-basis records for a qualified tax professional or crypto tax tool.

Can I swap WBTC on networks other than Ethereum?

WBTC-like assets and wrapped versions can exist on other networks, but this page's canonical route is Ethereum WBTC. If your balance is on another chain, verify the exact token contract, bridge origin, liquidity, and supported Uniswap chain before signing.
